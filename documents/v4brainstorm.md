# Dhikr v4.0.0 — 100 Unique & Useful Feature Ideas 💡

This document contains 100 unique, non-trivial, and useful ideas for v4.0.0 of the Dhikr app. In accordance with the project guidelines, all ideas relating to **prayer times** and **Qibla direction** have been excluded, focusing strictly on Quran study, spiritual mindfulness, customization, voice/audio accessibility, premium widget concepts, and local data utilities.

---

## 📖 Category 1: Quran Study & Hifz Tools (1-15)

1. **Hifz Sandbox (Active Memorization Mode)**  
   Hides parts of an Ayah dynamically while the user recites or types to test their recall. Tap hidden words to reveal them.
2. **Ayah Connection Mapper**  
   A visual map connecting verses with similar themes, commands, or stories across different Surahs (e.g., linking verses about patience).
3. **Root Word Dictionary**  
   Tap on any Arabic word in the Quran to see its root letters, literal meaning, and all other occurrences of that root in the Quran.
4. **Tafseer Compare Mode**  
   Compare classical interpretations (e.g., Tafsir Ibn Kathir and Tafsir al-Jalalayn) side-by-side on tablet/phone layouts.
5. **Asbab al-Nuzul (Contextual Timelines)**  
   An interactive timeline linking historical battles and events to the exact verses revealed during them.
6. **Tajweed Rule Highlighter Control**  
   Custom toggles to highlight specific rules (e.g., Ikhfa, Ghunnah, Qalqalah) with custom color selections in the Quran reader.
7. **Quran Vocabulary Flashcards**  
   Dynamic spaced-repetition flashcards of high-frequency Quranic words to help users learn the language of the Quran.
8. **Quranic Grammar Analyzer**  
   Color-coded breakdown of syntax (nouns, verbs, particles) for advanced Arabic language learners and students of knowledge.
9. **Sajdah Tracker**  
   A logger specifically for tracking Sujud al-Tilawah (prostrations of recitation) and their references, ensuring users do not forget them.
10. **Quranic Subject Index Explorer**  
    Tree-view search directory categorized by subjects (e.g., science, ethics, inheritance, stories) leading directly to relevant verses.
11. **My Quran Journal**  
    A personal reflection journal linked directly to individual verses, allowing users to type personal notes, commitments, and thoughts.
12. **Spaced Repetition Hifz Revision Planner**  
    Algorithmically schedules Surahs/Juz for revision based on how well the user remembered them during previous review sessions.
13. **Ayah Comparison Engine**  
    Bookmark two or more verses from different Surahs to compare thematic structures or specific vocabulary differences.
14. **Daily Quranic Quiz Generator**  
    Multiple-choice questions generated from translations, vocabulary, and historical contexts of recently read Surahs.
15. **Mutashabihat Finder (Similar Verses Detector)**  
    Highlights verses that look almost identical (Mutashabihat) to help Hifz students avoid mixing up verses during recitation.

## 📿 Category 2: Dhikr & Spiritual Mindfulness (16-30)

16. **Breathing & Dhikr Sync**  
    A visual circle that expands and contracts, guiding the user to synchronize subhanallah, alhamdulillah, or allahu akbar with their breathing.
17. **Intentional Silence Timer**  
    A timer that blocks all app sounds and notifications, dedicating 2-5 minutes to silent contemplation (Muraqabah) before starting Dhikr.
18. **Custom Azkar Recipe Builder**  
    Create sequential routines (e.g., "Morning Travel Routine": 3x Ayat al-Kursi, 3x Falq, 3x Nas, 33x Tasbih) and run them as a continuous flow.
19. **Smart Wearable Integration (Haptic Tasbih)**  
    Connect to smartwatch companion apps to allow incrementing counts using the watch bezel, volume buttons, or screen taps.
20. **Tasbih Completion Soundscapes**  
    Subtle background ambiance (rain, wind, stream) that dynamically gets clearer/softer as you approach your target.
21. **Mood-Based Dhikr Selector**  
    Suggests specific supplications based on emotional state (e.g., anxious, grateful, grieving, tired).
22. **Interactive Tasbih Beads (Physics Mode)**  
    Swipe down realistic virtual beads that bounce with sound and physical feedback, mimicking a real Tasbih.
23. **Salawat Counter (Durood Tracker)**  
    A dedicated sub-tracker celebrating Durood/Salawat, especially optimized with alerts and targets for Fridays.
24. **Dhikr Speed Tracker**  
    Analyzes target pacing to alert users if they are reciting too fast, encouraging mindful, slow recitation.
25. **Istighfar Depth Log**  
    Keep track of days dedicated to seeking repentance, with accompanying reminders on the conditions of sincere repentance.
26. **Dhikr Streak Safeguard (Sadaqah System)**  
    If you are about to lose a streak, you can bypass it by completing a specific daily challenge or reading a special morning/evening series.
27. **Names of Allah Audio Guide**  
    High-quality audio pronunciations and vocalization guidance for each of the 99 Names of Allah.
28. **Ruqyah Self-Treatment Mode**  
    A guided recitation layout for standard Ruqyah verses with translation and instructions on usage.
29. **Spiritual Heart Monitor (Self-Reflection Check-in)**  
    A daily 3-question survey checking spiritual focus, tongue safety, and heart intentions, charting the results weekly.
30. **Remembrance Audio Player**  
    Loop beautiful, melodious recitations of daily Adhkar (Morning/Evening) in the background with synced translations on the lock screen.

## 👥 Category 3: Community & Sharing Features (31-45)

31. **Group Dua Circles**  
    Create private, link-based circles with friends/family to join in reciting a collective target (e.g., reading Quran together or 10,000 Salawat).
32. **Prophetic Wisdom Cards Creator**  
    Generate beautiful, stylized, shareable typography graphics of Hadith or Ayahs using modern templates.
33. **Local Mosque Board Integration**  
    Let users manually add a link/feed to their local community noticeboard to fetch announcements/community events.
34. **Silent Dua Request Feed**  
    A bulletin where anonymous users can request dua. Other users can tap "I prayed for you" to provide encouragement.
35. **Spiritual Gift Dispatcher**  
    Package a selection of Quran verses, Duas, and animations and send them to friends as a digital gift.
36. **Joint Hifz Challenges**  
    Join global, public (anonymous) leaderboards focusing on chapters memorized or pages read.
37. **Sunnah Action of the Week**  
    A global collaborative goal where all app users try to revive a specific Sunnah (e.g., visiting the sick, smiling, charity).
38. **Family Spiritual Dashboard**  
    Keep family members motivated by sharing goals, streaks, and achievements in a private circle.
39. **Charity Goal Sync (Sadaqah Helper)**  
    A micro-ledger that helps you log and track the money you put aside for charity, tracking targets.
40. **Dua Journal Export**  
    Export your personal Dua requests and answered prayers as a beautifully formatted PDF to share or print.
41. **Anonymous Reflection Board**  
    A space where users post notes on how a specific verse helped them through a difficult day.
42. **Halal Habit Buddies**  
    Pair up with an accountability partner within the app to keep each other active in daily habits.
43. **Islamic Heritage Travel Log**  
    A user-curated database of historic mosques, archives, or shrines, showing geographical historical contexts.
44. **Daily Gratitude Wall**  
    Add 3 things you are grateful to Allah for every day, building a searchable history of blessings.
45. **Mentorship/Hifz Review Circle**  
    Allow trusted teachers to view student reading logs or checklists to monitor their memorization progress remotely.

## 🔊 Category 4: Audio, Voice & Accessibility (46-60)

46. **AI Pronunciation Checker**  
    Uses device microphone to listen to your recitation of short Surahs and highlight potential pronunciation errors.
47. **Offline Audio Pack Downloader**  
    Granular management of reciters, letting users download audio for single Surahs, Juzs, or individual verses.
48. **Dynamic Recitation Speeds**  
    Change playback speeds (0.5x to 2x) without altering the pitch of the Qari's voice.
49. **Verses Loop Controller**  
    Select a range of Ayahs and loop them a specific number of times (e.g., play verse 1-3 five times) to assist in memorization.
50. **Background Playback & Lock Screen Widgets**  
    Full integration with native OS media controllers to display Arabic text, translation, and controls on the lock screen.
51. **Voice Commands Control**  
    Perform actions (e.g., "Start Tasbih", "Open Surah Yaseen", "Play Al-Mulk") hands-free.
52. **Screen Reader Optimizations (Accessibility Mode)**  
    Complete semantic styling and screen-reader accessibility for visually impaired users.
53. **Haptic Feedback Language**  
    Distinct vibration patterns for different actions (e.g., different buzzes for reaching 33, 99, 100, or when starting a new section).
54. **Recitation Sync-Scroll**  
    Autoscrolls the Quran text layout exactly in sync with the audio recitation.
55. **Multi-Reciter Compare**  
    Play the same verse spoken by different reciters (e.g., Al-Afasy, Al-Husary, Abdul Basit) to appreciate different recitation styles (Tarteel/Tajweed).
56. **Dynamic Font Scaler**  
    Double-pinch gestures directly on the Quran text to scale fonts instantly.
57. **High Contrast Themes**  
    Themes specifically optimized for outdoor reading, seniors, or users with specific visual needs.
58. **Translation Voice Over**  
    Audio recitation of translations alongside the Arabic text for hands-free listening.
59. **Whisper Mode Timer**  
    Plays audio recitations very softly, easing the user into sleep with Surah Al-Mulk or Sajdah.
60. **Word-by-Word Audio**  
    Tap on a single word to play its individual audio pronunciation immediately.

## ⚙️ Category 7: Advanced Data Sync, Customization & Integrations (86-100)

86. **Local Backup Encryption**  
    Add password protection (AES-256) to exported backup files before uploading to cloud services.
87. **Theme Designer Studio**  
    Create custom app color schemes, select custom typography, and modify layout paddings to fit your exact taste.
88. **Dynamic App Icon Changer**  
    Change the app launcher icon dynamically based on your achievement milestones or current themes.
89. **Third-Party Calendar Integration**  
    Sync your Islamic calendar directly with Google Calendar or Apple Calendar, adding reminders for fasting days.
90. **Smart Notifications (Sadaqah/Dua Alerts)**  
    Reminders that trigger when you enter specific regions, like a reminder to send peace upon the Prophet when passing near cemeteries.
91. **Data Analytics Export**  
    Export all raw statistics (Dhikr count history, prayer logs, reading time) as a CSV file for personal analysis.
92. **Dhikr API Webhooks**  
    Integrate Dhikr counts with external tools or automation scripts (e.g., trigger smart home lights to change color on reaching target).
93. **Local Sync (No Internet Backup)**  
    Backup and restore data locally over Wi-Fi between two devices without using cloud servers.
94. **Multiple Profiles Support**  
    Support multiple user profiles on a single device, useful for families sharing an iPad or Android tablet.
95. **Widget Custom Backgrounds**  
    Upload your own calligraphies or images to serve as backgrounds for home widgets.
96. **Tagging System for Bookmarks**  
    Add customizable tags (e.g. #Hope, #Patience, #Forgiveness) to bookmarks for quick searching.
97. **Dhikr Sound Effects Store**  
    Choose from different tap sounds (wooden beads, digital clicks, metallic rings, soft water drops).
98. **Text Copy & Formatting Tool**  
    Easily copy verses with customizable styling (e.g., wrap in brackets, append translation, include Surah/Ayah number).
99. **Focus Mode (Deep Study)**  
    Temporarily disables all sections of the app except the Quran page, muting system notifications to help you focus.
100. **Custom Widget Action Builder**  
    Configure exactly what happens when you tap different parts of a widget (e.g., tap top opens Quran, tap bottom opens settings).
