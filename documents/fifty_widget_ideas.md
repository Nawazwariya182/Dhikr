# 50 Premium Home Screen Widget Ideas for Dhikr App

This document outlines 50 new widget concepts designed to enhance spiritual connectivity, learning, and tracking directly from the Android home screen.

## 📖 Category 1: Quranic Study & Verse Reflection

| ID | Widget Name | Grid Size | Update Cycle | Tap Actions | Description & Visual Layout |
|:---|:---|:---:|:---:|:---|:---|
| **01** | Ayah Reflection Prompt | 4x2 | Daily | Open App Reflection | Displays a short Ayah with a reflective question (e.g., *"How can you apply this today?"*). |
| **02** | Hifz Memorization Card | 3x2 | Action-driven | Toggle blur / reveal | Blurs out part of a memorized verse. Tapping reveals hidden words to help with Hifz revision. |
| **03** | Sajdah Verses Reminder | 2x2 | Weekly | Open Quran | Highlights the next Sajdah (prostration) verse in the Quran with its context and rules. |
| **04** | Quranic Root Word of the Day | 2x1 | Daily | Root Lexicon | Displays a single Arabic root word (e.g., *R-H-M*), showing all derived words in the Quran. |
| **05** | Quran Vocabulary Builder | 2x2 | 12 Hours | Flip Card | Flashcard style widget showing an Arabic Quranic word. Tapping flips it to show English meaning. |
| **06** | Subject Explorer | 4x1 | Daily | Open Subject | Shows a topic (e.g., *"Patience"* or *"Charity"*) and rotates matching verses. |
| **07** | Reciter Spotlight | 3x1 | Weekly | Stream audio | Displays a featured reciter of the week with a quick play button to stream their recitation. |
